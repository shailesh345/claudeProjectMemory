### 6. Recommendations

**What Worked Well:**
- Creator's authentic voice resonated with audience
- Posting time (3 PM EST) was optimal
- Reel format outperformed static posts
- Hashtag #SpringVibes drove discovery

**What to Improve:**
- Link in bio could be more prominent
- Earlier content submission for better review time
- Consider longer campaign duration for sustained impact

**Similar Creators to Try:**
- @creator_similar_1 (same niche, higher reach)
- @creator_similar_2 (different style, same audience)
- @creator_similar_3 (emerging, lower cost)

**Optimal Posting Strategy:**
- **Best Days**: Tuesday, Wednesday, Saturday
- **Best Times**: 3-5 PM EST, 8-10 PM EST
- **Best Format**: Short-form video (Reels/Shorts)
- **Optimal Length**: 15-30 seconds

**Content Format Suggestions:**
- More behind-the-scenes content (high engagement)
- User-generated content reposts (authenticity)
- Tutorial/educational content (high saves)
